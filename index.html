Privacy Policy for Habit Spark

Effective Date: 9/19/2025

Habit Spark ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our app collects, uses, and safeguards your information. By using our app, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Habit Spark is designed to work fully offline, and no personal data is collected, stored, or shared on our servers.

The information stored in the app stays only on your device and includes:

  • Your habit entries
  • Progress tracking data
  • Notes and custom goals

We do not require an account or login to use Habit Spark.

2. Third-Party Services

Habit Spark displays ads using Google AdMob. These ads may collect anonymous usage data to provide relevant and personalized advertising.

  • Device information (e.g., model, OS version)
  • Approximate location data
  • Interaction with ads

For more details, please review Google's Privacy Policy.

3. How Your Data Is Stored

All your habit tracking data is stored locally on your device. We do not:

  • Upload your data to any servers
  • Share your data with third parties (except for ads as mentioned above)
  • Access your personal information remotely

If you uninstall the app or clear its data, all locally stored information will be permanently deleted.

4. Permissions Used

  • Storage: To save and manage your habit tracking data locally on your device.
  • Internet: Required only for displaying ads via Google AdMob.

We do not use these permissions to access or collect your personal files.

5. Children's Privacy

Habit Spark is not intended for children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can take action.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Effective Date" at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy regularly for updates.
